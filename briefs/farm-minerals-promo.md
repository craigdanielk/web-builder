# Brief: Farm Minerals CropTab Promo

## Business
Farm Minerals develops CropTab™ fertilizers that use carbon-capsule technology to
deliver nutrients with zero waste and zero emissions, positioning CropTab™ as a
compact tablet alternative to bulk fertilizer. The promo page markets early access
to CropTab™ products for the 2026 season.

**Reference URL:** https://www.farmminerals.com/promo
**Preset:** health-wellness

## What They Need
A single-page early-access landing page that persuades qualified growers and
agronomy partners to apply for the 2026 CropTab™ program and complete a detailed
eligibility form.

## Key Requirements
- Hero section with CropTab™ positioning ("Fertilizer, Reinvented"), a concise
  value proposition (100 kg reimagined as a 13 g tablet), and primary CTA
  ("Request access" / "Check eligibility")
- Problem framing section about fertilizer inefficiency and nutrient loss
  (including the "up to 70% of nitrogen is lost" claim)
- Science/technology differentiation section highlighting carbon-capsule delivery,
  internal uptake, and zero manufacturing emissions (Haber-Bosch bypass)
- Performance/impact stats section including: 4 continents tested, 100x lower
  transport cost, 0.5 ton CO₂ saved per acre, and ≥5× ROI with the existing
  disclaimer
- Environmental positioning section: "Cleaner for the planet — without the carbon
  cost," avoiding offset-based claims
- Logistics/efficiency section with the lighter-shipping message (13 kg box
  replacing 25 tons of fertilizer) and ~500–600 lb CO₂e savings per acre
- Field trials proof section(s) listing countries and crops currently testing
- “Next update” callout updated to **October 2026 — full-season biomass and ROI data**
- Formats & Access section listing early-access product formats and specs:
  CropTab™ NPK, Nutripeak™ Base, CropTab™ K, CropTab™ N, CropTab™ P, CropTab™ NPK+
- Closing urgency section reinforcing limited early-access slots and secondary CTA
- Footer with contact email (hello@farmminerals.com), LinkedIn, and minimal legal links
- Application form with required fields and a placement disclaimer

## Target Audience
- Large and mid-size growers looking for high-efficiency fertilizer programs
- Agronomy partners and trial collaborators evaluating new nutrient delivery systems
- Progressive farms interested in emissions reduction and logistics savings

## Brand Personality
- Scientific and data-first
- Performance-driven and pragmatic
- Environmentally responsible without sounding like marketing fluff
- Farmer-first, confident, and direct

## Specific Requests
- Keep CTA language: “Request access” and “Check eligibility”
- Preserve the existing claims and metrics exactly
- Maintain the early-access urgency and exclusivity tone
- Present trial data and geography in a transparent, real-results framing
- Use product imagery/illustrations that emphasize compact tablet formats

## Technical Notes
- Single-page responsive build with fast load times
- Form should include these required fields:
  - Full name
  - Farm name
  - Phone number
  - Farm location + country
  - Primary crops (multi-select)
  - Other crops
  - Required acreage (acres/ha toggle)
  - Season timing (Spring 2026, Summer 2026, Fall 2026, Winter 2027, other)
  - Current fertilizer program type (granular, liquid, urea/UAN, anhydrous,
    organic, foliar, other)
- Submission disclaimer: “Submitting this form does not guarantee placement.
  We review each application to maintain trial quality and regional balance.”
- No new integrations beyond form submission/validation and a success/error state
