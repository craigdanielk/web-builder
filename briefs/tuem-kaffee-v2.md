# Brief: Turm Kaffee v2

## Business
Turm Kaffee (Turm E-Commerce AG) is Switzerland's oldest premium coffee brand,
based in St. Gallen. They roast in small batches with Swiss precision and sell
coffee beans, ground coffee, capsules, tea, brewing equipment, barista tools,
and gifts. They also operate a Barista Academy offering hands-on courses.

**Reference URL:** https://shop.turmkaffee.ch/en
**Preset:** artisan-food

## What They Need
A full marketing homepage that communicates their heritage, showcases their
product range, promotes the Barista Academy, and drives e-commerce sales.
This is a rebuild of their existing Shopify storefront with a modern,
elevated design language.

## Key Requirements
- Hero must establish Swiss heritage + premium positioning ("Switzerland's oldest
  premium coffee brand" energy — but don't use that phrase verbatim)
- Weekly/promotional offers section with countdown timers and product cards
- Clear product category navigation: Coffee (beans, ground, capsules, specialties),
  Tea, Machines, Accessories, Gifts
- Two featured product category callouts (Coffee and Tea) with imagery
- Craft story section — 3-panel editorial showing roasting philosophy, freshness
  commitment, and daily coffee ritual elevation
- Barista Academy promotion — clear CTA driving to courses
- Contact section with physical address (Turmgasse 8, 9000 St. Gallen),
  phone (+41 71 221 20 60), email
- Certification/association badges section
- Newsletter signup for offers and coffee world content
- Multi-language support indicators (DE/FR/EN)
- Free shipping threshold messaging (CHF 90+)
- Payment method display (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Klarna, Mastercard, Visa, etc.)

## Target Audience
- Swiss coffee enthusiasts and daily drinkers
- Home baristas looking for premium beans and equipment
- People interested in barista training and coffee education
- Gift shoppers looking for quality Swiss coffee gifts
- Multi-language audience across German, French, and Italian-speaking Switzerland

## Brand Personality
- Swiss precision meets artisan warmth
- Heritage-proud but modern in presentation
- Knowledgeable and passionate without being pretentious
- Inviting — coffee as a daily ritual and social moment
- "Made with precision, roasted with passion"

## Specific Requests
- Dark, rich hero section with coffee imagery — warm lighting, not cold/clinical
- Marquee/ticker banner showing quality values (Swiss quality, Freshly roasted,
  Barista Academy, Fine selection of teas, etc.)
- Product category browsing should feel visual and inviting, not like a catalog
- The craft story section should use alternating image/text layout with warm
  photography of roasting process, beans, and barista moments
- Barista Academy section should feel like an invitation, not an advertisement
- Footer should include country/currency selector, language selector, newsletter,
  social links, legal links, and payment icons
- Overall feel: premium Swiss craftsmanship, warm earth tones, generous whitespace

## Technical Notes
- Shopify storefront (existing — this is a frontend rebuild)
- Multi-currency: CHF primary, EUR for Austria/Germany
- Multi-language: German (primary), French, English
- Must be fast and mobile-optimized
- Payment integrations: Apple Pay, Google Pay, Klarna, Mastercard, Shop Pay,
  Union Pay, Visa, American Express
